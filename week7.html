CS373 Summer 2015: Daniela Reyes

Week 7:

Jul 26 2015

It's sad to realize that this class is coming to an end. However I'm glad I don't have more projects keeping me at GDC unil 6 am. This is a though class, but it is totally worth it. Even though you have a shorter amount of time during the summer you still get to learn a lot of different tools.I now have a much better understanding of Python, Javascript, HTML, AngularJs and JQuery.

I will definitely take another class with Professor Downing. He is knowledgeable on the material and really patient when answering questions. However I still don't like the daily quizzes. They make sure to test the cases that are completely different to the other ones. They are pretty tricky and you have to make sure to know the material. So studying 15 min before class is not a good idea, which sadly it was sometimes all the time I had.

My favorite part of this class was probably the last project. Even though it consumed most of my time it was really interesting. I learn a lot when working on it, and it also gave me several ideas for some personal projects. Our web ended up looking pretty good. However I think it would had been nice to had an extra day just to fix small details and make sure the website look better.

Tip-of-the-week:
One of my partners for the last project found a Firefox's tool call Style Editor. It lets you “view and edit all the stylesheets associated with a page”. I wish we had found it earlier, it would had made this project much easier.
The following link has a small description of the tool:
https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Tools/Style_Editor
